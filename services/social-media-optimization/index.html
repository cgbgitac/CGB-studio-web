What is Social Media Optimization (SMO)?

SMO assists you:

  • Boost visibility and discoverability
  • Enhance engagement metrics (likes, shares, comments, saves)
  • Enhance follower quality and retention
  • Maintain a strong and consistent brand voice
  • Drive more traffic to your website or landing pages
  • Convert followers into leads and customers

In short, SMO bridges the gap between presence and performance.
